Chicken Parmesan

Description

Imagine a pizza. Now imagine that instead of bread, the base is made of fried chicken. Sounds awesome right? Well that is what Chicken Parmesan is. A crispy, fried chicken cutlet topped with sweet and tangy marinara and stretchy cheese.

The following recipe is based off of Brian Lagerstrom's website: www.brianlagerstrom.com

Ingredients

Chicken

  • chicken thighs
  • salt
  • all-purpose flour
  • eggs
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • black pepper
  • olive oil
  • provolone cheese
  • fresh mozzarella
  • grated parmesan cheese

Marinara

  • olive oil
  • minced garlic
  • dried chili flakes
  • tomato paste
  • can of crushed tomatoes
  • sugar
  • salt
  • fresh basil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken generously with salt and let it rest.
  2. Pound the chicken gently with a saucepan.
  3. Prepare three containers for the flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumb mixture respectively (breadcrumbs, parmesan, pepper, salt)
  4. Coat the chicken thoroughly in flour, shaking off the excess. Dip in the beaten eggs. Press into the breadcrumb mixture. Make sure that the entire chicken is thoroughly covered in breadcrumbs.
  5. Let the chicken rest to ensure that the breading sticks during the deep fry.
  6. Meanwhile, heat a saucepan and add olive oil.
  7. Add the minced garlic and chili flakes.
  8. Stir in tomato paste.
  9. Add crushed tomatoes, sugar, and salt. Let the mixture thicken.
  10. Finally, add the basil.
  11. Heat olive oil in a large saute pan. Lay the breaded chicken carefully and fry until both sides are golden brown.
  12. Transfer the fried chicken to a wire rack.
  13. Spread marinara sauce over the fried chicken. Place a slice of provolone cheese and three slices of fresh mozzarella. Finally, sprinkle in grated parmesan cheese.
  14. Place the cutles unver a broiler until the cheese is melted and browned.
