Arroz con Pollo

Description

Rice is such a nice, comforting food. But a great way to make it more interesting rather than relaxing is to add a variety of spices. This Mexican rice dish does just that. Paired with an equally spicey piece of oven-roasted chicken and you have yourself a highly enjoyable meal.

The following recipe is based off of Brian Lagerstrom's website: www.brianlagerstrom.com

Ingredients

Chicken

  • chicken thighs
  • olive oil
  • coarse salt
  • black pepper
  • paprika
  • ground cumin
  • turmeric
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder

Rice

  • jasmine rice
  • cumin
  • garlic powder
  • turmeric
  • onion powder
  • coarse salt
  • chunk salsa
  • water
  • frozen peas
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Mix chicken thighs with olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, cumin, turmeric, onion powder, garlic powder.
  2. Roast the chicken on a sheet tray with a wire rack sprayed with pan spray until the skin is crispy and the meat is cooked through.
  3. In a deep pot, add and heat up olive oil, then saute jasmine rice.
  4. Add in cumin, garlic powder, turmeric, onion powder, and salt.
  5. Stir in the salsa.
  6. Add water and bring to simmer. Add peas, cover with a lid, and bake in the oven alongside the chicken.
  7. Fold cilantro into rice.
